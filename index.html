How to Host an Epic Watch Party on Disney Plus: A Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to hosting a watch party on Disney Plus, we've got you covered. Below are a few straightforward steps designed to guide you through the process and ensure you can start streaming with friends in no time

  • Installing the Extension The first step is to install the Disney Plus Party extension. You can easily do this by clicking here. Ensure you have a compatible device, such as a Windows, macOS, or Chromebook laptop/PC. Remember, use a supported browser like Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge for a smooth installation process
  • Pin the Extension to Your Toolbar Next, pin the watch party extension on your smart device. Then, you must click on the extension icon in your browser's toolbar. Then, click the pin icon to secure the Disney Plus Party Chrome extension to your toolbar for easy access.
  • Access Your Disney Plus Account Once you've pinned the extension, the next step is to access your Disney Plus account. Ensure you have an active subscription to take full advantage of the extension. If you're new to Disney Plus, set up your account first. Otherwise, log in and get ready to join or host an exciting watch party.
  • Host a Disney Plus Watch Party Step four is to create your Disney Plus Watch Party. After that, start by signing into your Disney Plus account and choosing a video to stream with your friends. Once the video plays, click the “DP” button on your toolbar. Then, select the “Start the Party” option to generate a watch party URL. Share this URL with your friends and loved ones to invite them to join the fun!
  • Join a Disney Plus Party Complete your Disney Plus Party setup by joining your watch party with friends and family. First, install the Disney Plus Party extension and log in to your Disney Plus account. Next, click on the watch link shared by your friend or host.
